Some frequent questions

Ingredients

What's the difference between the green and black tea?



Both the Black and Green tea are made from the same leaves of one plant - Camellia Sinensis. The difference between them is the process of the leaves. The difference between the colors are the results of natural fermentation, after leaving the previously wilted and rolled leaves on the air. While the black tea gets fermented, the green tea won't, it gets steamed almost immediately after plucking and then rolled up into different shapes, keeping the natural greenish colors.




Are herbal teas similar to green and black teas?



If we look at the process of making, then more or less yes. The ingredients are of course different, they won't come from the Camellia Sinensis, but from different herbs. The most famous ones are Chamomile, Peppermint.


Making and Keeping

How to serve tea the best way?



One of the best ways to serve tea is from a teapot. You also need to pay attention to the material which the pot is made from, as it can influence the taste. The best materials are porcelain and glass. In porcelain cups you can reach the best taste of a tea. The best temperature to drink it is 60 Celsius.


Sometimes teas are bitter. What's the reason behind it?



We recommend boiling the water for more than 5 minutes - with this the calcium and magnesium can leave the water. With this method we can reach a better quality compared to the cases where we use just-now-boiled water.



Tea and Health

I heard that it contains caffeine. Is it true?



Every black and green tea has caffeine in it - circa 30 mg/cup. But we also have the fruit teas and herb teas that doesn't contain caffeine, and in the ORDER page you can see all the Pickwick teas that other counties can offer - and there you can also find some of them, that doesn't have any caffeine at all!



Do normal black teas contain any aromas?



No. To our normal black tea we won't add any aroma, it's 100% natural.



Do Pickwick teas contain artificial aromas?



No, we don't have any tea that contains anything artificial.
